CHINESE SPICY RICE RECIPES:

INGREDIENTS:
  • Chicken 1/2 kg
  • Rice 1/2 kg
  • Cabbage 1 smail
  • Carrot 1
  • Peas 1/2 cup
  • Spring onion 1
  • Green chillies 3.4
  • Egg 2
  • Salt 1 TBSP
  • Chinies Salt 1 (TBSP)
  • Black Pepper 1 (TBSP)
  • Whole Spice Ground 1 (TBSP)
  • Soya Sauce 3 TBSP
  • Yellow Food Colour 1 Pinch
  • Oil 1/2 Cup

METHOD:
  1. Boil chicken with pinch of salt and remove bones.
  2. In a non sticking pan take (2 TBSP) of oil fry cabbage and carrot untill cooked,don't overcook .now put boilled peas andd spring onion.stir for few minutes and set a side.
  3. Now heat (1/2) cup oil in(preferably)non sticking pan.add (2) baten eggs are mix vigorously,now add food colour
  4. Put chicken in it and stir for some time then put cooked vegetables.
  5. Add green chillies and all spices from salt to soya sauce, mix well.
  6. now put boiled rice stirand shake well by covering and holding the pan from sides so that all things mix well.
